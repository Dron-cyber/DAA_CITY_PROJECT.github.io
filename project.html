About the City – TANURA

Tanura is a futuristic smart city conceptualized over 1500 sq. km, designed from the ground up to demonstrate sustainable development, intelligent infrastructure, and efficient urban management. The city incorporates advanced algorithm-driven systems to optimize mobility, resource usage, and public services.

Phase 1 focuses on establishing a structured urban layout, defining key components, classifying functional zones, and planning the interaction between various algorithmic systems. This phase builds the foundation for solving real-world urban challenges through data structures, graph algorithms, and intelligent automation.
